**[Analysis]** Switching Costs and Stickiness:

- **Do-Nothing Prevalence:** For state/local infrastructure, budget constraints and procurement complexity mean ~20–30% of identified projects are deferred or cancelled—a material risk to backlog conversion.

**Procurement Blocker:** The primary blockers are (1) security clearance timelines for new C&GS work (12–24 months for Top Secret), and (2) federal budget appropriations timing (continuing resolutions delay contract awards by 3–9 months).
