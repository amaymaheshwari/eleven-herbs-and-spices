## SECTION 10: PRICING POWER AND ELASTICITY TESTING

### Pricing Governance

*Fact.* HRI uses a **dynamic yield-management system** (similar to hotel revenue management) that sets daily/weekly/monthly rates based on local utilization, equipment availability, and competitive data. Rate floors are set at branch level; national account rates are negotiated centrally.

### Elasticity Evidence

*Analysis.* Post-COVID (2021–2023), HRI achieved significant rental rate increases (+8–12% y/y in peak periods) as fleet supply was constrained and demand surged. As fleet supply normalized in 2024, rate growth moderated to approximately 2–4% y/y — indicating **moderate pricing power that is highly cyclical and utilization-dependent**.

### Packaging Strategy

*Analysis.* HRI's ProSolutions packaging bundles equipment + operator training + compliance documentation + maintenance at a premium to bare-metal rental. This specialty bundling generates estimated 20–35% higher revenue per OEC dollar than general rental and is the primary pricing-power lever.
